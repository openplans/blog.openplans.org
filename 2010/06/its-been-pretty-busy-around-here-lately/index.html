It’s been pretty busy around here lately

Posted on June 3, 2010 by Vanessa Hamer

But good news all around:

  • OpenGeo will be helping OBIS, the world’s largest marine biogeographic database, move to an open source mapping platform.
  • Tomorrow at Personal Democracy Forum, Phil will be talking about OpenMuni and the intersection of open data, open source, and municipal governments.
  • Kim and Rebecca are at Vélo Québec, leading a workshop on bringing livable streets issues into classrooms.

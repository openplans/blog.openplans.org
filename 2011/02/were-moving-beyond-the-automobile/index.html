We’re Moving Beyond the Automobile

Posted on February 8, 2011 by Vanessa Hamer

Streetfilms just launched the trailer for their Moving Beyond the Automobile series.Â Each Tuesday over the next ten weeks, they’ll be posting a new chapter about smart and proven strategies to reduce traffic and improve street safety for all users. Hope you will tune in.

The series tackles a whole range of important topics, from Bus Rapid Transit to Congestion Pricing to Car Share, showing how each can help people to use cars less – or not at all.

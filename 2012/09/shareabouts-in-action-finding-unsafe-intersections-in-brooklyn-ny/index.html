Shareabouts in action: finding unsafe intersections in Brooklyn, NY

Posted on September 30, 2012 by Naama Lissar

Make Lafayette Avenue Safer is expanding its successful Fort Greene campaign to other parts of Brooklyn. Their Shareabouts map will document intersections that make your walking and biking trip better or worse.

All comments gathered on the map will be coordinated for Community Boards, local elected officials, and police precincts, to make positive changes and to make Brooklyn safer. If you know of an unsafe intersection, make sure it's on the map: makebrooklynsafer.shareabouts.org

On Shareabouts Day, the OpenPlans team worked with five community organizations to set up custom crowdsourced maps.  Shareabouts is an advanced crowdsourcing platform for public input and data gathering. Find out more and explore demos at shareabouts.org, or contact us to discuss our hosted Shareabouts solutions for cities

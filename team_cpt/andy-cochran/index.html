Andy Cochran

Creative Director

Andy provides the creative vision for a range of successful projects. He designs user interfaces for tools like Shareabouts, he creates the look & feel for (and builds) websites like this one and Streetfilms, and he’s responsible for OpenPlans’ identity/branding.

Andy enjoys drawing, painting, writing/recording music, and Alton Brown-inspired cooking. He loves living in New York City, even though his heart is buried in the bluegrass of Louisville, KY. He likes his art contemporary, his food spicy, and his country music old-timey. His ridiculously ginormous music collection is comprised mostly of quirky indie-rock, twee, and Hank Williams Senior albums—his favorites existing on vinyl, of course.

Andy holds a BFA from the Maryland Institute College of Art, and he has over a decade of experience in design, both print and web. You can follow him @andycochran.

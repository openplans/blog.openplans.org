Jon Tehrani

Research Fellow

Jon feels very lucky to be able to work in a capacity which perfectly aligns with his passions, specifically developing innovative tools which enable citizens to take a vocal and active role in planning the future of the places they call home.

A graduate of Emory University, Jon completed two Americorps terms of service and is one semester away from obtaining a Master’s degree in Community and Regional Planning from Temple University. Jon enjoys cooking and all food related activities, pickup basketball in the park, and wandering around the city of Philadelphia with a camera in his hand.

Where OpenPlans will be during Philly Tech Week

Posted on April 7, 2014 by Aaron Ogle

Philly Tech Week is upon us and Mjumbe and I will be out enjoying many of the great events. Here is where we’ll be:

Economic Impact of the Technology Sector

Monday, April 7, 12pm: A candid discussion about Philadelphia as a home for tech companies.

OpenAccessPhilly

Monday, April 7, 3pm: A special PTW edition of the original civic fusion meetup.

Tech Mix It Up: A Diversity Networking Affair

Monday, April 7, 5pm: Death to brogramming!

Exploring Civic Volunteering with Technology

Thursday, April 10, 8am: Kicking off Commit, a connecting volunteer technologists with non-profits

N3rd St BBQ

Friday, April 11, 2pm: A BBQ with IndyHall and friends — our people.

Watch the myPhillyRising app in action

Posted on February 10, 2014 by Ellen McDermott


The City of Philadelphia’s Office of Innovation and Technology has released a great video showing how the myPhillyRising app works.

An open source project started with OpenPlans, and launched for public use last fall, the myPhillyRising app connects Philadelphia residents to events, resources, and neighbors in their communities. The video is part of the city of Philadelphia’s larger digital strategy that aims to engage residents in new ways.

Bike-to-transit crowdsource map wants your input

Posted on October 1, 2012 by Anthony Denaro

via Plan Philly – The Bicycle Coalition of Greater Philadelphia, Delaware Valley Regional Planning Commission and three regional transit agencies want to know, and to get feedback they have launched a bike-to-transit crowdsource map. The map-based survey lets users click on a specific station and tell whether or not they currently bike to the station, if the station has adequate bike parking and if they would bike if parking were improved.
